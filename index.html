AMAZING FEATURES

Our primary focus was on building an app that allows you to quickly and easily search and browse the items from online thrift shops with zero gimmicks.

Categories

Category lists show you whats available along with how many subcategories exist (if any).

Ending time

Most items in the app are on auction. So we've moved the ending time to the top.

Bids

In auction listings, total bids will be listed clearly and don't miss the Make Bid button.

Shipping

At this time, shipping is handled by the location that is selling the item. Shipping info is listed under the item image.

Price

Price is what you pay. Value is what you get.

Top 50

See what's trending via the top 50 listing. See the main menu for more.
